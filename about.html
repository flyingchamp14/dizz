What is Dizz?




Welcome to Dizz! At Dizz, we aim to make every long drink of lemonade available anywhere, anytime! Our signature smiley tablets are unique in which you can drop them in water, wait, and watch as the tablet slowly fizzes into your favorite drink: lemonade! After that, all you have to do is sit back and drink up the joy. Have a lemon-y day!
